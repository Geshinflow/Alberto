About Alberto Watch Company

Founded in the heart of Nigeria, Alberto Watch Company has been crafting timeless pieces since 2001. With a passion for precision and an eye for detail, our founders, Alberto and James sam, envisioned creating watches that were not just timekeepers but symbols of elegance and heritage.

From the rolling hills of Geneva to the bustling streets of Zurich, our watches have adorned the wrists of connoisseurs and enthusiasts alike. Each watch we create tells a story, a testament to the relentless pursuit of perfection and the rich tradition of Swiss watchmaking.
